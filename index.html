Meet Our Doctor & Our Philosophy

Dedicated care right here in the heart of Mini-Cassia.

Dr. Jake Sanford, DC

Dr. Jake Sanford is a dedicated and passionate chiropractor committed to serving the Mini-Cassia community. He earned his Doctor of Chiropractic (DC) degree and is highly skilled in a range of modern techniques, from traditional spinal adjustments to advanced soft tissue modalities like dry needling.

Dr. Sanford believes in a patient-first approach, taking the time to understand each individual's unique health goals. His focus is not just on treating symptoms, but on empowering patients with the knowledge and care needed to live an active, pain-free life.
